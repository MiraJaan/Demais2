Tips
- Air Quality Tips

When Air Quality is POOR

  • Avoid outdoor exercise, especially running or cycling
  • Wear a mask if you're stepping out
  • Keep windows and doors closed

When Air Quality is MODERATE

  • Light outdoor activities are okay
  • Drink more water to reduce throat irritation

When Air Quality is GOOD

  • Best time for outdoor sports and walks
  • Open windows for fresh air

Indoor Air Tips

  • Keep rooms well ventilated
  • Clean fans and filters regularly
